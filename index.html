Introducing 'tamara'

'tamara' uses high quality air purifying grade Activated Carbon to help you manage odours in your car, refrigerator and closed encloses like cupboards, shoe racks etc.

The fresheners that we use today emit nice odour masking all the bad odours, so we continue to inhale the bad odours but with a good smell. They don't help us with not inhaling the various gases that get into our blood stream through inhalation.

Deviating from the concept of masking the odours, 'tamara' adsorbs (yes,'adsorbs') odours on to its high porous surface area leaving the area free from odours. This way it reduces the various gases that get into our body through inhalation.

Directions for use

1. Remove the 'tamara' bag from the sealed package.
2. Each bag is of size 3 inches (height) x 2 inches (width) and can be easily placed or tagged as per convenience.

It lasts upto 6 months! And a little over too depending on the extent of odours it is exposed to.

Each 'tamara' bag is packed in high quality air tight resealable packing. This way, it remains fresh until it gets across to you. It is children safe and pet friendly.
It is not for internal consumption though.

All good things also come to an end, so when you notice that the effectiveness of the 'tamara' bag is reduced, place the bag in direct sunlight for about an hour or so and it will last you another couple of weeks until you can get your fresh set of 'tamara' bags.

After use: Since the content of the 'tamara' bag is 100% organic, the used 'tamara' bags can be cut open and the content inside it can be put into earth or plant soil, so it goes back to nature.
